who we are

Creative and unconventional designers

来自中国本土的原创设计师品牌“YOOWOOOD铕物”是深圳市有物时装有限公司旗下的女装品牌。该品牌于2020年成立，命名灵感由人们在表示雀跃情绪下而发出“哟呼”的口语化演变而来。

“YOOWOOOD铕物”在理念上传递现代、个性、自由的穿衣形式，来打造无服装禁忌的穿搭风格，改变古板的成衣印象，在无年龄界限的定位里为当代时装产业发展挖掘更多面的可能。

The original designer brand "YOOWOOOD" from China is a women's clothing brand under Shenzhen Yoowoood Co., Ltd. The brand was established in 2020, and its naming inspiration evolved from the colloquial expression of "Yo Hoo" expressed by people.

"YOOWOOOD" conveys modern, personalized and free dressing forms in concept to create a dressing style without clothing taboos, change the stereotyped impression of ready-made clothes, and explore more possibilities for the development of contemporary fashion industry in the positioning of no age limit.
